This research uses the US COVID-19 & Human Mobility (2021) dataset to anticipate daily_cases in order to solve a regression problem. To ascertain the connection between human mobility (such as employment or transit activities) and the virus's transmission, the model looks at 935,442 rows of data. This project's goal is to forecast illness epidemics in the future by using current social behavior.

DataSet: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/amroaskar/us-covid-19-and-human-mobility-2021/data

Kind:Daily_cases is the regression target variable. What is anticipated to be learned or predicted by the model? Retail and recreation, supermarket and pharmacy, parks, transit hubs, offices, and residential areas are examples of mobility measures.
